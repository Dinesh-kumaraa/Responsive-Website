A Feast for All Senses.
Authentic Food, Timeless Architecture, & Delightful Snacks.

Welcome to Chettinadu Paradise, your gateway to the rich and vibrant world of Chettinadu. We are dedicated to offering an authentic experience that encompasses the finest elements of this historic region, known for its exceptional cuisine, timeless architecture, and delightful snacks.

Our Authentic Foods

At Chettinadu Paradise, we offer an authentic taste of Chettinad with a variety of foods that showcase the rich flavors and traditional spices of the region.

Aappam With Coconut milk

Savor the delicate flavors of our Aappam, a soft and fluffy rice pancake with a lacy, crisp edge, served with creamy, aromatic coconut milk. It’s a delightful combination that captures the essence of traditional Chettinad comfort food.

Seeraga Samba Mutton Biryani

Indulge in our Seeraga Samba Mutton Biryani, where fragrant Seeraga Samba rice is perfectly cooked with tender mutton and a blend of rich, aromatic spices. Each bite delivers a unique and flavorful experience that celebrates traditional Chettinad cuisine.

Mutton Uppukari

Experience the bold flavors of our Uppukari, a tantalizing dish of succulent meat sautéed with aromatic spices and a touch of salt. Each mouthful offers a perfect balance of savory and spicy, embodying the essence of Chettinad’s culinary tradition.

Our Timeless Architecture

Discover Chettinad’s timeless architecture, where intricate carvings and elegant design reflect the grandeur of a bygone era. Each structure is a masterpiece, showcasing the rich cultural heritage and architectural brilliance of Chettinad.

Living Area

Explore the living areas of Chettinad houses, where spacious, open courtyards and intricately designed wooden pillars create a harmonious blend of elegance and comfort. These traditional spaces embody the charm and grandeur of Chettinad’s architectural heritage.

Meeting Hall

Step into the majestic meeting halls of Chettinad houses, where grand pillars and ornate decor create a space of timeless elegance. These halls, designed for communal gatherings, reflect the rich heritage and architectural splendor of Chettinad.

Traditional Door

Admire the exquisite architectural doors of Chettinad houses, adorned with intricate carvings and vibrant colors that tell stories of tradition and artistry. Each door is a stunning entryway into the rich heritage and craftsmanship of Chettinad architecture

Our Delightful Snacks

Enjoy Chettinad’s delightful snacks, where each bite offers a burst of traditional flavors and crispy textures. From savory murukku to sweet athirasam, these treats are perfect for indulging in the authentic taste of Chettinad.

Thenkuzhal Murukku

Enjoy the crispy perfection of Chettinad's Murukku, a savory snack made from a blend of rice flour and spices. Each bite offers a delightful crunch and a burst of traditional flavors.

Brown Sugar Athirasam

Enjoy the sweet, crispy delight of Athirasam, a traditional Chettinad snack made from rice flour and jaggery. Each bite offers a perfect blend of caramelized sweetness and crunchy texture, celebrating the essence of Chettinad’s culinary heritage.

Seedaikai

"Experience the irresistible crunch of Chettinad’s Seedai, a classic snack crafted from rice flour and a blend of spices. Its golden, crispy exterior and flavorful, savory bite make it a beloved treat in Chettinad cuisine.
